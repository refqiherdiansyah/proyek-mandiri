About Me

I am a third-semester student in the English Education program at Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang with a strong passion for Product Management and a solid background in web development. My journey into Product Management began with an intensive four-month bootcamp at Smartpath, where I gained comprehensive experience in product lifecycle management, strategic planning, and cross-functional team leadership. In addition to my Product Management training, I have developed a robust skill set in web development, including proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and advanced frameworks such as React and Angular. This combination of skills enables me to bridge the gap between product strategy and technical execution effectively.

My expertise in Product Management extends to conducting in-depth market analysis, defining clear product visions, and creating detailed roadmaps that drive product success. I excel in collaborating with stakeholders to refine product features, prioritize development tasks, and ensure alignment with business objectives. My web development skills enhance my ability to contribute to product design and implementation, allowing me to oversee both strategic and technical aspects of product development. I am proficient in using project management tools like JIRA and applying agile methodologies to streamline workflows and achieve project milestones.

Throughout my professional journey, I have adopted a results-driven approach, focusing on delivering high-quality, innovative products and enhancing user experiences. My experience includes leveraging data-driven insights to inform decision-making, adapting strategies to meet market demands, and leading teams to successful product launches. I am committed to ongoing learning and professional development, continuously seeking to expand my knowledge and skills in both Product Management and web development. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects that drive growth and success in dynamic and evolving markets.
