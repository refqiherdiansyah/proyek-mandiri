About Me

I am a third-semester English Education student at Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang with a strong passion for Product Management and a solid background in web development. My journey into Product Management began with a four-month bootcamp at Smartpath, where I gained experience in product lifecycle management, strategic planning, and team leadership. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Angular, which allows me to effectively bridge product strategy and technical execution. My expertise includes market analysis, defining product visions, creating roadmaps, and using tools like JIRA with agile methodologies. I focus on delivering high-quality, innovative products and enhancing user experiences, and am committed to continuous learning and professional growth to drive impactful projects in evolving markets.
